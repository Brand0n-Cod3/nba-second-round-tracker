NBA SECOND ROUND DRAFT ANALYSIS

A study of all NBA second-round draft picks from 2000 to 2024 and how their careers turned out in the long run.

Research Question

Are second-round draft picks worth it in the long run?

Hypothesis

The second round is primarily comprised of players who end up doing very little in the league, with few exceptions that are usually international or right after the first round.
